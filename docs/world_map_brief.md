# Pantheon Age World Map Brief

This brief is for image-generation models. It should be used to generate a
textless concept map of the Pantheon Age world.

## Core Shape

Pantheon Age takes place in a bounded world surrounded by the Void Wall. Current
civilization cannot cross the Void Wall. Beyond it lies endless void and the
realm of Outer Gods.

The main landmass is the Northern Continent. A partial eastern coast of the
Southern Continent may appear near the bottom or southeast of the map.

## Seas

- The western sea is the Chaos Sea. It lies between the western island kingdom
  and the western coast of the Northern Continent.
- The central inland sea is the At Sea, a Mediterranean-style trade sea between
  the Northern and Southern Continents.
- The eastern sea route is the Golden Coast, leading toward the wealthy lands of
  the Southern Continent.
- The At Sea and the Golden Coast are connected only by the narrow Golden Gate
  Strait.
- The Golden Gate Strait is controlled by Selemeria and is the most important
  trade choke point in the known world.

## Countries

- Albion: western island kingdom, naval power, finance, world navy, located in
  the Chaos Sea west of the Northern Continent.
- Lumiere: western Northern Continent, republic, scholarship, museums,
  revolution, truth research.
- Wald: northern Northern Continent, industrial land power, railways, military
  industry, Ironblood Church.
- Ost: central-eastern Northern Continent, multiethnic empire, imperial court,
  judgment church, music, old aristocracy.
- Isteria Crownlands: southern Northern Continent, merchant-oligarchic monarchy,
  inland royal capital, coastal merchant oligarchs, navy, trade.
- Noctia: hidden neutral religious city-state in the central mountain range.
- Rosvia Grand Duchy: northeastern Northern Continent, cold, vast, autocratic,
  death faith.
- Selemeria: southeastern Northern Continent, controls the Golden Gate Strait,
  Abyss faith, unavoidable trade partner despite being viewed as heretical by
  other powers.

## Mountains

The Astra Mountains sit around the central border region between Lumiere, Wald,
Isteria, Ost, and Noctia. Noctia is hidden within these mountains.

## City Symbols

Use city symbols only. Do not write labels.

Albion:

- world largest financial capital and great port;
- industrial city;
- Ocean God church headquarters.

Lumiere:

- world second largest white city;
- truth research city near the Astra Mountains;
- southern/coastal resort port.

Wald:

- capital;
- huge western industrial zone;
- river-border port city.

Ost:

- imperial capital;
- industrial minority city;
- At Sea port city.

Isteria:

- inland royal capital;
- art and finance city;
- western At Sea trade port and fertility church headquarters.

Noctia:

- hidden city-state in the mountains.

Selemeria:

- capital at the Golden Gate Strait and Abyss church center.

Rosvia:

- cold northeastern capital.

## Visual Rules

- Generate an original western fantasy map.
- Do not use real-world coastlines.
- Do not add any text, names, labels, numbers, legends, or readable writing.
- Use parchment texture, dark brown ink lines, antique gold decoration,
  hand-drawn mountains, rivers, sea waves, ships, trade routes, city icons, and
  mystical border effects.
- The Void Wall should appear as a mysterious boundary around the world.
